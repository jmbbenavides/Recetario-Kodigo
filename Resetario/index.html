Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

4 large eggs

room temperature

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher sal

2 tablespoons unsated butter

Intructions

  1. Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.
  3. Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.

  5. When the eggs are mostly set but still soft, fold in half.
  6. Slide the omelette onto a plate
